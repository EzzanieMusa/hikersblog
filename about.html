Welcome to Hikers Blog

In this blog are about 2 hikers who experience hiking in Bukit Sarang Helang and hiking trails in Bukit SiPatir which is the latest addition to the hiking spots in Brunei.

Both are very popular, great places of adventure for people of all ages and a very convenient spot for hikers of Bandar Seri Begawan.

About Me

We’re Sofhie and Ezzanie, local Bruneians who shared the same passion - hiking. We’re active travelers who love to hike, go on road trips and learn about new places. Wherever we go, we seek out local experiences and cultural understanding. We created this site to help you plan your next trip and to document the best culinary hiking destinations in the world.
